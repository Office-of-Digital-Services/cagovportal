The centers are busiest in the middle of the day. Come earlier or later to avoid waiting.

More about these centers: Los Angeles County Disaster Recovery Centers

Services on Site

Here are the services you can expect at the centers:

  • State and local services:

    Sign in at the door. Services include:

    • Food and food benefits
    • Health services and advice
    • Unemployment
    • Disability benefits
    • Replacing personal documents
    • Tips for hiring contractors
    • Help with insurance claims
    • Tax help and relief

Federal services

Sign in at the FEMA tents. Services include:

If you can, bring these items with you:

  • Housing
  • Home Repairs
  • Legal Services

Before you arrive

Use this checklist to plan your in-person visit.

  • Address of the damaged primary home
  • Insurance coverage information
  • Current phone number and mailing address
  • Bank account information

When you get here

  • Parking: Free parking available. People may be there to direct you.
  • Sign in: Sign in at FEMA tents for federal benefits, or go into the building for state and local services.
  • Restrooms and beverages available.
  • Use the Plan your in-person visit to help you find the services you need.
