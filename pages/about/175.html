The Future Happens Here, First

As America nears the 250th anniversary of the signing of the Declaration of Independence, California approaches its own significant milestone.

In 2025, we celebrate 175 years of shaping the future.

Much of the most profound progress in society over the past 175 years has roots in California.

California is the world’s leader in technology and agriculture, arts and entertainment, and progress and innovation. From enshrining farmworker rights and LGBTQ+ rights, to being home of the first woman U.S. Speaker and the first woman Vice President – California is a state of dreamers and doers.

There is still more work to do as we continue to build a more perfect union – but it cannot be denied: the future happens here, first.
