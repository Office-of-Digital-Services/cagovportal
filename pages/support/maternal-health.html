Get access to comprehensive maternal health information and support services. Learn how to navigate pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period with better access to care, mental health resources, and essential programs.

The Office of the California Surgeon General created this list of helpful services.

Financial assistance

CalWORKs

Department of Social Services

This program gives cash aid and services to eligible California families in need.

Child Support

Department of Child Support Services

Child support services to provide children with money towards living and medical expenses

Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Program

California Department of Community Services and Development

This program helps low-income families with their home energy bills.

Low-Income Weatherization
Program

California Department of Community Services and Development

Program provides solar photovoltaic (PV) systems to reduce energy costs

Food assistance

CalFresh

Department of Social Services

This program provides food assistance to people who meet the income requirements.

Women, Infants, and Children (WIC)

California Department of Public Health

WIC helps families get healthy food. It also provides info on nutrition, breastfeeding, and referrals.

Health care

Medi-Cal

Department of Health Care Services

Medi-Cal offers free or low-cost health coverage for eligible Californians.

Abortion access

California Department of Public Health

Find current and accurate information about abortion access in California.

Comprehensive Perinatal Services Program (CPSP)

California Department of Public Health

Services for pregnant people from conception through 60 days postpartum (Medi-Cal benefit).

Black Infant Health Program (BIH)

California Department of Public Health

An equity program to support pregnant and postpartum Black mothers.

Early Start

Department of Developmental Services

Intervention services for infants and toddlers with developmental delays or at risk for having a developmental disability.

Mental health support

Maternal Mental Health

California Department of Public Health

Learn about maternal mental health support and resources, including maternal suicide prevention.

BrightLife Kids

California Department of Public Health

Personalized behavioral health coaching for parents, caregivers, and children 0–12.

CalHOPE

Department of Health Care Services

Free peer counseling when people feel stressed, anxious, depressed, or worried.

California Home Visiting Program (CHVP)

California Department of Public Health

For overburdened families who are at risk for Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs).
