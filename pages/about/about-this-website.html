CA.gov is the official website for the State of California. You can find and access California services, resources, and more.

The Office of Digital Services (ODS) team runs this website. ODS is part of the California Department of Technology. Hundreds of state departments contribute and update information on this site.

History

The CA.gov portal first launched in 1996 when Governor Pete Wilson was in office. It contained information about:

  • The Governor
  • History, culture, economy
  • Doing business in California
  • Government officials and agencies
  • Disaster preparedness

It started as an initiative in 1995. The State Librarian and the California State Home Team created the original site.

Over time, the website grew and contained more information, including links to public services.
