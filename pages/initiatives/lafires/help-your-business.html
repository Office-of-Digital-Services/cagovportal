Find support and assistance for local business recovery

Small business support

by Small Business Administration (SBA)

The Small Business Administration (SBA) provides a variety of resources to help small businesses and entrepreneurs including financing, training, and advocacy.

Apply for emergency and disaster assistance for employers

by Employment Development Department (EDD)

The Employment Development Department (EDD) offers services to support employers impacted by disasters including disaster relief payments, request an extension for payment and payroll reports.

Taxes for businesses

Business income taxes

by Franchise Tax Board (FTB)

Businesses impacted by the wildfires qualify for a postponement to file and pay income taxes until October 15, 2025. Only Los Angeles County was granted postponement. If the principal place of business is outside of Los Angeles County, you must file and pay by the normal due dates.

Emergency sales tax or fee relief

by CA Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA)

Get relief from penalty, collection cost, and interest. You can also get copies of tax records and up to a 3- month filing extension on your tax returns.
